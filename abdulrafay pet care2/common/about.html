About Us

Learn more about FurShield and our mission

Our Story

FurShield was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create a comprehensive platform that connects pet owners, veterinarians, and animal shelters to provide the best care possible for every animal.

What started as a small project by a group of animal lovers has grown into a thriving community dedicated to improving the lives of pets and their owners through technology, education, and compassion.

Today, FurShield serves thousands of pet owners, hundreds of veterinarians, and dozens of animal shelters across the country, making pet care more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone involved.

Our Mission

To create a world where every pet receives the care, love, and protection they deserve through innovative technology and community collaboration.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform that connects all aspects of pet care, making it easier for pet owners, veterinarians, and shelters to work together for the well-being of animals.

Our Values

Compassion, Innovation, Integrity, Collaboration, and Excellence in everything we do for the animals and people we serve.
